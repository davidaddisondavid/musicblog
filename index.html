Front Page

Welcome to the beginning of a website.
I don't know what this will end up being, but for now there's a blog and a weekly-updated list of gigs happening in London.

I named the website after the EP Howl or Buzz Under The Influence of Heat by the Minutemen, honestly because I was looking through what I'd listened to recently, and thought that it made for a cool name. But, if I was lying, I'd say that it's because I want this website to be like a punk zine, something made out of love and obsession for music, people and places â€“ held together with all the html knowledge I've scraped together in the last few weeks.
I hope that this site can be your life.

This week I'm obsessed with the song 'Trem 2' by Mission of Burma. It's based around an undulating guitar riff played through a tremolo pedal, intertwined with a disco drum pattern: there's lots of noise and punk rock sharpness, but it's also so so groovy.

I'm also loving a new york band called tal castle: their music is very playful, with a bunch of instruments and quirky production. it's full of soul and arranged beautifully, with tonnes of space and atmosphere.
